Fungal Turfs are one of the Turf items acquired by digging Fungal Turf tiles with a Pitchfork. They are only found in caves and come in three different colors : blue, red and green. Variants of Mushtrees and Mushrooms can be found in these turfs and covers the ground of a Mushtree Forest. Like other turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.


Trivia

  • Currently, red and green fungal turfs are referred to as the standard "Fungal Turf".
