“ I might hold off the inevitable a bit longer with this. ”

–Wendy

The Log Suit is a wearable Armor found in the Fight tab. It requires 8 Logs and 2 Ropes to craft and a Science Machine to prototype. It has 450 durability and absorbs 80% of physical damage. It can be used to refuel Campfires and Fire Pits, even when its durability is nearly gone.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, the Log Suit can be used to craft the Scalemail.

Recipe

Trivia

  • When paired with the Football Helmet, it will absorb 96% of incoming damage. It's best to put it on last because of the damage calculation of armor and since it's easily renewable.
  • Despite being acceptable fuel for Camp Fires and Fire Pits, a dropped Log Suit will not catch fire from burning objects or rogue campfires and Stars.
