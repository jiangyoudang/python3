A Tumbleweed is a form of flora found in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. Tumbleweeds can be found in the Desert biome tumbling around, often in groups. Catcoons can also spit up Tumbleweeds. When the player is near a Tumbleweed, it will make an almost musical sound. The player can harvest a Tumbleweed and get 3 of any of a number of loot items, including any of the Gems, making some previously non-renewable items renewable. Note that food gathered from Tumbleweed are not always fresh.
