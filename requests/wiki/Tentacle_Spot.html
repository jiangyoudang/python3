Tentacle Spots are a rare item with a 20% drop chance from a killed Tentacle and a 40% chance from a killed Big Tentacle. They can be used to craft the Feather Hat and the Rain Coat, and Wickerbottom can use them to craft the Book "On Tentacles". Tentacle Spots do not spoil and can also be burned as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.
