Electric Milk is a Dairy Food item dropped by a charged Volt Goat in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. It can be consumed as is, or used as an ingredient for recipes in the Crock Pot, such as Ice Cream.


When consumed raw it restores 10 , making it a good sanity restoring item even when raw.
