A Light Bulb is obtained by picking a Light Flower or dropped by a Slurper or a Big Tentacle. It can be used to build and refuel a Lantern or Miner Hat by 19% per bulb. Light Bulbs can also be eaten to restore 1 Health.

Light bulbs can be placed on the ground to make a Light source big enough to prevent an attack by Charlie.

Since Light bulbs spoil quickly, only grow underground, and give off light even when left alone, it's best to leave Light Flowers unpicked until their bulbs are needed to refuel.
