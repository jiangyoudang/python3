Testing Tools & Cheat Keys (commonly referred as Testing Tools) was a Mod developed by Don't Starve community member clockwork. It would provide an alternative of Console so that players could spawn every entitie and item in the game conveniently. Also, players could utilize the mod to create adventure maps for other players.

Abandoned and replaced with Mini Tools .
