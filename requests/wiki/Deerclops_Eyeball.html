The Deerclops Eyeball is an uncookable Food item added in the A Winter's Tale update. Pigs and Spiders will eat it if it is on the ground, and Bunnymen will consider it as meat and attack the player on sight if they happen to be carrying one. The eye does not spoil and cannot be Cooked. When eaten, it drains 15 Sanity but grants 60 Health and 75 Hunger.

A Deerclops Eyeball is used to craft a Houndius Shootius and the Eyebrella.
