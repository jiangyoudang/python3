The Crank Thing is 1 of 4 Things used on the Wooden Thing to travel to the next world. It is surrounded by evil flowers in a small Savanna along with a pig house surrounded by 3 Spiky Trees and an Axe.



Trivia

  • Wilson indicates that it is "tough enough to withstand the most intense experiment".
  • According to Maxwell, it is used to "agitate the humours".
