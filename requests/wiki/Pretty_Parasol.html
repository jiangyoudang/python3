–Wickerbottom

The Pretty Parasol is a craftable item from the "Reign of Giants" DLC. It is easier to craft than an Umbrella, but provides less protection from rain. It does however, restore a very small amount of sanity while equipped. During the Summer, the Pretty Parasol can lower rate of Overheating while equipped. It does not have durability, but instead spoils over time similar to the Garland.
