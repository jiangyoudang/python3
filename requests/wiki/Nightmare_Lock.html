–Maxwell

The Nightmare Lock is a special adventure mode object used to trap the person sitting on the Nightmare Throne. It looks like a tiny version of the Wooden Thing with spikes surrounding it. It can only be found at the epilogue of the Adventure Mode beside Maxwell. Also, a Divining Rod is required to unlock the lock. After unlocking, Maxwell will be released and the player's character will take his place on the throne, back in Sandbox Mode the player will become Maxwell but will keep the items he had when he entered Maxwell's Door.

Trivia

  • In older versions of the game, the Shadow Key (which shared the same model as a Purple Gem) was used to free Maxwell.
