Waffles are a Fruit Food item created in a Crock Pot by adding at least 1 Butter, 1 Egg or Tallbird Egg, and 1 Berry. It takes 10 seconds to cook. The Waffles provide more Health but satisfy less Hunger than their constituent ingredients.

It should be noted that adding a Mandrake can result in Mandrake Soup.
