A Stinger is an item commonly dropped by a Bee or Killer Bee when killed. It has no function on its own, but it's used to craft a Sleep Dart.

Reign of Giants

With Reign of Giants active, you can harvest Stingers by destroying Tumbleweed.

Usage

Trivia

  • Although the stingers on the Bees are very small, they appear larger when dropped or picked up.
