Carpeted Flooring is a craftable Turf Item. This flooring can be crafted with 1 Beefalo Wool and 1 Board, and an Alchemy Engine is required to prototype it.

Players may also dig up the flooring with a Pitchfork around Maxwell Biome, for example, a Wooden Thing's spawning location. Although 1 Beefalo Wool is used to craft the carpet flooring and while Beefalo Wool is mainly used to craft hats or vests for heat during the winter, it is important to note that the Carpeted Flooring does not provide the player with any warmth and so as a result does not have an insulation factor. 

Trivia

  • Carpeted Flooring was introduced in the A Little Rain Must Fall update.
  • Before the Hungry For Your Hunger update, Carpeted Flooring required the Shadow Manipulator to prototype.
  • It is one of four craftable turfs (the others being Cobblestones, Wooden Flooring, and Checkerboard Flooring).
  • Eyeplants can't spawn on Carpeted Flooring.
  • In older versions of Don't Starve, Carpeted Flooring could be found underneath Maxwell's Door, along with Marble Pillars.
