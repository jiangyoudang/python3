Sandy Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging sandy tiles with a Pitchfork. It is one of the turfs that covers most part of a Desert. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.
