Autumn is one of the four seasons from the "Reign of Giants" DLC and has a 50% chance to be the starting season instead of Spring. Autumn is the calmest of the seasons, where the player doesn't have to worry about Overheating or Freezing, and there is only occasionally Rain, it is comparable to Summer from the vanilla version of the game. Autumn lasts 20 days by default so the first Autumn lasts either from day 1 to 20, or from day 37 to 56. This will change if you edit the length of the seasons in the Preferences. Stages in Adventure Mode may have different seasonal variations. 

Autumn is mostly a peaceful season, comparable to Summer from the vanilla version of the game, with not much dangers in it. During Autumn, most creatures are active, Farm Crops grow normally, and Food is easier to find. However, any resource such as Grass, Saplings, or Berry Bushes that withered in the summer will return to their harvested state at the start of Autumn. That means these resources will still not be immediately available at the start of autumn.

During autumn, the MacTusk N' Son Hunting Party are not present at their Walrus Camp. Red Hounds may occasionally appear rather than Blue Hounds during timed Hound attacks; Redbirds and Crows appear; and the Koalefant appears with a red hide.The event that sets Autumn apart from the other seasons more than any other is that the Bearger may be encountered who will eat food off the ground and destroy any Structures and Plants that get in the way. Also during Autumn Birchnut Trees will start to lose their leaves, which change color and fully-grown ones will yield more Birchnuts when cut down.

Autumn leads to Winter so players might want to spend their first Autumn preparing for the harsh weather that will soon be upon them.
