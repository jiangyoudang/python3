The Caves are a dangerous place, but they can also be worthwhile. You can use the Caves to obtain Nightmare Fuel, Carrots, and other resources fairly easily.

Contents

[show]

    Preparation

    Before farming the caves it is recommended to bring several things. First of all, bring a lot of crafting materials, such as a stack of grass and twigs, as well as quite a few logs. This will be invaluable underground. Also recommended is a Miner Hat, as this will allow you to have light around you whilst keeping your hands free for fighting or gathering more easily.

    Section 1: Nightmare Fuel

    Nightmare Fuel is an essential resource in the late-game. It is used to make many different things such as the Amulet, which can be used to revive you.

    1. Find some mushtrees.
    2. Chop them down. Eat the blue mushrooms which they drop but make sure not to cook them.
    3. Continue to do this until your sanity is low enough for hallucinations to spawn. Attack these hallucinations for Nightmare Fuel.
    4. Alternatively, drain your sanity below 30. This will make the hallucinations attack you instead of you having to chase them down and then kill them.

    Section 2: Bunny Puffs, Carrots, and Boards

    Quite a few resources can be easily obtained by smashing down Rabbit Hutches with a hammer.

    1. Locate some Rabbit Hutches.
    2. Use your hammer to smash them down. Each Rabbit Hutch will drop 5 Carrots, 2 Boards, and 2 Bunny Puffs.
    3. A word of warning: any nearby Bunnymen will attempt to eat the carrots, so make sure to gather them quickly.

    Section 3: Beard Hairs

    Beardlords will drop 2 Beard Hairs each, but beware of their horde behavior and the fact that they do 60 damage per hit.

    1. Find some Bunnymen.
    2. Lower your sanity below 80,
    3. All Bunnymen will transform into Beardlords, which can then be killed.
