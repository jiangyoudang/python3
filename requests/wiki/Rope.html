“ Tightly woven coils of usefulness ”

–Wilson

A Rope is a Refined Item crafted with 3 Cut Grass. A Science Machine is required to unlock the recipe. Ropes can be used to craft various Tools, Survival items, and Weapons.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC there is an alternative method of getting Rope, by destroying Tumbleweed.
