Pig Heads are decorative structures usually found around Touch Stones, Rundown Houses, and Merm villages. They are the severed heads of Pigs on sticks, similar to Merm Heads, the latter being often found in Pig Villages.

Destroying a Pig Head with a Hammer drops 2 Pig Skin and 2 Twigs. The player cannot build a Pig Head.

Reign of Giants

In Reign of Giants, during a Full Moon, the Pig head's eyes will open and be pure white, similar to a Buzzard's eyes, and it will generate an insanity aura. Destroying a Pig Head during this time yields Nightmare Fuel.

Trivia

  • Pig Heads were added in the Doorway to Adventure update.
  • Pig Heads can be found around the Merm camps, and Merm Heads can be found around the Pig Villages. This might represent a war between Pigs and Merms.
  • When examined by Wilson, he says, "Looks like an offering to the beast", a reference to the book Lord of the Flies by William Golding, where a group of stranded children end up killing pigs and putting their heads on sticks as an offering to "the beast", an imaginary monster. Wendy also references Lord of the Flies when she says, "Kill the pig! Spill his blood!", one of their chants.
  • The way the Pig Heads glow during a Full Moon in Reign of Giants looks similar to the way they were shown to glow when using a Touchstone in one of the update trailers.
