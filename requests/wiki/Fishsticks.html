Fishsticks are a Meat Food item produced in a Crock Pot by combining at least 1 Fish or Eel and only 1 Twig. It takes 40 seconds to cook it. Fishsticks are particularly good for restoring a large chunk of health without using any expensive resources or having to create a more expensive Crock Pot recipe such as Dragonpie.

It should be noted that putting more than 1 Twig in the recipe will result in Wet Goop. Placing two Monster Meat is acceptable as Monster Lasagna can not have Twigs. Adding Corn will result in Fish Tacos.
