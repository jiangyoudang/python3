“ It will keep the sun off my head. ”

–Woodie

The Straw Hat is a wearable Dress item. It costs 12 Cut Grass to craft. It is required to construct the Miner Hat and the Bush Hat. Despite Woodie's examination line (seen above), the Straw Hat has no beneficial effects for the player in vanilla Don't Starve, and it is worn purely for aesthetic reasons.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, the Straw Hat provides a small protection from wetness and overheating when worn. It loses durability.

Usage

Trivia

  • It used to cost 30 Science Points to craft, and had a value of 10 Science Points when used on a Science Machine  in the research days of Don't Starve.
  • In the Reign of Giants DLC, the simple combination of using the Straw Hat and the Umbrella is enough to reduce the player's Wetness during most rains, being an easy and accessible way out for the Wetness problem as the Straw Hat has a low cost based solely on Cut Grass.
