The Hammer is a tool used to deconstruct structures such as chests or walls, or as a weapon. It requires 3 Rocks, 3 Twigs, 2 Ropes (or 6 grass in Reign of Gaints) to craft and a Science Machine to prototype. Hammers can break down any player-made or naturally-occurring structure such as Pig Houses, yielding at least 50% of the materials used to create the structure in most cases. If the player Hammers a chest, all items will fly out.

Usage

To use the Hammer to break down a structure, simply right-click the Hammer on the structure or equip it by right-clicking from the inventory and then right-click on the structure you intend to destroy. It can be used as a weapon simply by left-clicking while having it equipped, and deals 17 damage. It takes several strikes for the Hammer to break something down, and the spacebar or right mouse button must be held while swinging. This tool has 75 uses, meaning each time the player hits a structure or attacks, it will lose 1.33% durability.

Note that using the hammer on a structure that has "uses" (e.g. the Tent) may return less than 50% of the materials used to craft it, based on how many times the structure has been used. This was presumably added so that players can no longer exploit the game (e.g. by smashing the tent with the hammer after 5 uses). So for structures that have usage, for each type of ingredient, the amount returned is:

original_amount * percent_uses_left * 50%.

Note that at least one of each type of ingredient will always be returned as a minimum.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, the Hammer can be used to stun Moleworms.

Trivia

  • It used to cost 30 Science Points to craft, and it used to have a value of 1-10 Science Points depending on its durability when used on a Science Machine.
  • Before the Caves update, the Hammer was used with left click. It was changed to right-click so less accidents would happen, such as hammering chests.
  • When Wilson examines the hammer, he says, "Stop! It's time! To hammer things!" reference to M.C. Hammer's "U Can't Touch This".
  • A wooden hammer is the icon for the Structures Tab.
  • Sometimes when hammering certain things, the resources will be shot out of the structure rather than being dropped as normal.
