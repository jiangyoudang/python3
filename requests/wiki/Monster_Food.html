Monster Foods are food items which have a monster food value causing them to create Monster Lasagna when more than one is used in a Crock Pot. Monster foods are typically poisonous and cause damage to Health and Sanity when eaten, however these effects can be lessened by cooking them. Eating 4 Monster Foods will also cause Pigs to be transformed into Werepigs. There are currently 7 types of monster food:

  • Monster Meat / Cooked Monster Meat / Monster Jerky
  • Durian / Extra Smelly Durian
  • Monster Lasagna

Trivia

  • There was an 8th Monster Food called Deadly Feast. It has since been removed, but is still available using the DebugSpawn code. Eating it will instantly bring the character's health to 0, though it acts like a regular Monster Food for Pigs.
