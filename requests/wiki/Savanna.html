Savanna (also referred to as Plains) is a type of Biome is rich with Grass and Mobs, while containing little flora. It is the only known Biome to spawn Beefalos, but it is also host to many Rabbits, Redbirds and Crows. Spider Dens may also rarely appear. Flowers are sometimes found, which spawn Butterflies.

While a lot of Grass Tufts to grow on this terrain, Evergreens don't spawn on a Savanna, unless the Savanna is close to another terrain on which Trees can grow. It is, however, possible to manually plant these Trees on a Savanna. The Crank Thing is always located in a small Savanna biome.

A Savanna can differ in size from a few tiles to an entire island, but Beefalo don't tend to spawn unless there are at least around 25 tiles present.

Tips

Savanna is usually the best Biome to settle in due to abundance of ever needed Grass, renewable food sources, and close proximity to Beefalo, while other essential renewable resources such as Twigs and Evergreens can be easily transplanted from other Biomes.
