Guano Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging up Guano tiles with a Pitchfork. This turf can commonly be found and covers a partial area on Stalagmite Biome and Rocky Plains . Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit. It is belived to be turf Batilisks have pooped on, as it is often found in Cave areas where Batilisks roam (and in fact, covered in Guano).
