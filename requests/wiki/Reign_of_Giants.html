Don't Starve : Reign of Giants

Developer Klei Entertainment
Platform Windows, Mac OS X, Linux, PS4
Release Date PC : April 30th 2014
PS4 : TBA
Distribution Download Only
The Reign of Giants is a Don't Starve DLC announced on January 15th, 2014 and released on April 30th 2014 on PC (Windows/Linux/Mac) and at a later, to be announced date on PlayStation 4.

A simplified Early Beta version was released to forum users playing through Steam on February 27th 2014 and, after two updates, an official Beta was released to all Steam users on April 2nd.

    Access

    The DLC can be accessed when starting a new game. At the 'New Game' menu there will be a checkbox to activate "Reign of Giants" content. To access any of the features of the DLC (including character unlocking) the box must be checked when starting the game. Players can still play vanilla worlds by unchecking the box.

    Tweaks/Changes to existing content:

    • Captured small animals (Rabbits, Birds etc.) now starve to death if not fed.
    • Deerclops can now freeze opponents and has a smaller attack range.
    • Many existing equippable items now offer differing amounts of water resistance.
    • Many existing items and structures are now flammable.
    • Both the Piggyback and Krampus Sack are waterproof (items inside don't become wet) and the Piggyback slows you down less.
    • The Umbrella now loses durability when protecting from rain and the Straw Hat when equiped.
    • The Heat Stone and Winterometor were renamed to Thermal Stone and Thermal Measurer, respectively. The Thermal Stone now can be cooled in an Ice Box or Snow Chester (This version of Chester is not exclusive to the DLC).
    • Lightning can now hit and damage the player.
    • Food ready to be harvested on Crock Pots and Drying Racks will start to spoil and some existing meals give warmth when consumed.
    • Many Set Pieces have a higher chance to spawn in Sandbox Mode. Frog Rain can also happen now in that mode.
    • Full Moon Nights are now bright and will offer protection from Charlie. Also during a full moon, Ghosts will come out of Graves, Flowers will turn into Evil Flowers, and Mushrooms will turn into Mushtrees.
    • During Spring, flowers will spawn on their own, Bees will become aggressive and Beefalo will be constantly in heat.
    • Clockwork Monsters and Maxwell Biomes are now more numerous.
    • Many items and structures have new recipes and crafting tiers.
    • Skeletons and Bones can now be hammered for Bone Shards.
    • Weather effects now happen in Caves and Ruins too.
    • Many items considered non-renewable can be made renewable with Tumbleweed. This includes Thulecite (requires Thulecite to begin with), Gems, Fireflies, Gears, and Gravedigger Items.
    • Additional options were added to the World Constumization menu. Adventure Mode and the different presets were tuned to fit the RoG changes.
    • Trees now offer protection from overheating and rain.
    • Glow Berries and Deerclops Eyeballs are now stackable.
    • The Ham Bat now does less damage as it spoils.
