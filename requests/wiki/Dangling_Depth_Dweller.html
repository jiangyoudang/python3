The Dangling Depth Dweller is a variant of Spider found in the Ruins. It is a white-colored variant of the regular Spider, with a pointed head and red eyes. They drop from the ceiling when the player steps on top of their web.

When attacking, Dangling Depth Dwellers first drop themselves from the ceiling using their silk. On the ground, they can leap at enemies in the same way as Spider Warriors. Their sticky webbing are common to be found in Sacred and Labyrinth along with Ornate Chests. Their nests are stuck on the ceiling and unreachable, so they cannot be destroyed.

Dangling Depth Dwellers have the same drops as all Spiders: Monster Meat, Spider Gland, or Silk.

  • Shadow Creatures that spawn from Nightmare Lights can activate the Dweller's web, which if left unchecked will spawn a big number of spiders over time.
