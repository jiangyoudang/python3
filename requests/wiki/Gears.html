Gears are items dropped by Clockwork Knights, Clockwork Bishops and Clockwork Rooks, can be found by digging up Graves (chance of 3.07%), and have a chance of dropping from Tumbleweed.

Gears can be used to build an Ice Box and a Divining Rod, as well as boost the max stats of WX-78 by simply consuming them. In the Ruins, repairing Broken Clockworks with 3 Gears will spawn a friendly damaged chess monster (Damaged Bishop, Damaged Knight, or Damaged Rook).

Clockwork Knights, Clockwork Bishops, and Clockwork Rooks will not respawn, nor will graves after being dug. Therefore, Gears are a non-renewable resource.

Reign of Giants introduced Tumbleweed which has a chance of dropping gears. This means gears are renewable, but only with Reign of Giants enabled.
