The Shelmet is a Armor. It's an uncommon drop from the Slurtle, with a 10% drop chance.

It has 750 durability and absorbs 90% of physical damage. It's the second strongest head armor, with 450 less durability than the Thulecite Crown.

Trivia

  • The name "Shelmet" is a portmanteau of "shell" and "helmet".
