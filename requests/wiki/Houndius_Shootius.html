The Houndius Shootius is a craftable ancient turret.

When placed, the eye will watch the player when they are close to it. It will drain Sanity by -2 per minute when nearby. It is passive and will not attack the player but will attack any mobs that the player attacks first or any mob that attempts to attack the player, but cannot help defend against Shadow Creatures. The projectiles shot do 65 damage per shot and the turrets briefly emits some light when charging the shot. The Houndius Shootius can be attacked or killed by other mobs that it is currently shooting at and has 1,000 Health. The turret can be repaired using healing items, and will also passively regenerate health at a rate of 12 per second. It cannot be taken apart by either a Hammer or the Deconstruction Staff, making it more "mob" than "structure". The Houndius Shootius's main purpose is to be used as a type of defensive turret to protect a certain area and help the player kill mobs.

Due to the fact that the Ancient Guardian doesn't respawn, one can only build 4 Houndius Shootius in a world, unless the player brings a pre-built turret or Guardian's Horn with them through a Teleportato.

Trivia

  • The Houndius Shootius was added in the A Moderately Friendly Update.
  • It bears a resemblance to an Obelisk of Light from Command and Conquer.
  • 3 Houndius Shootius can kill a Deerclops, with minimal help from the player (100-200 damage). It may also be that none are destroyed, if the Deerclops cycles between them rather than focusing on one, however this is unlikely.
  • If a player attacks a turret, other turrets will start firing on turret, and the turret will return fire until one of them is destroyed or distracted.
  • The Houndius Shootius was originally called Minotaur Clopper. This was changed to Mino-Clopper, and finally its current name, Houndius Shootius.
  • The Houndius Shootius can be used in conjunction with a Bat Bat to heal quickly; its high health and passive regeneration makes it ideal to attack and heal, at the cost of sanity.

Bugs

  • If you kill the target of the Shootius while it's charging the shot, it will not fire and will keep emitting light until a new target is available.
  • Hitting the Houndius Shootius with a Ice Staff, Fire Dart or Fire Staff will cause it to attack the player.
