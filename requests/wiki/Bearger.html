The Bearger is one of the giants in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. The Bearger will appear in Autumn and seek out food to eat, even stashed food. Even though the Bearger will eat all varieties of food, it will only be sated by eating 10 Honey, after which it'll fall asleep.

It has two 'different' attacks, one in which it slams the ground with its front paws that will break trees, structures, and uproot plants in a large area around the bearger. The attack will also deal more damage to the player the closer he is to the center, if the player stands right next to the bearger it will deal massive damage.

The other attack is when it stands on its back paws and swipes at the player with one of it front paws. If the player stands close to the bearger, then it will swipe instead of hit the floor up to 3 times, and then the bearger will slam the floor once.

When in combat the Bearger will charge at the player if he is not in close range, destroying trees and structures in its way and then slam the ground when it gets close. If the player starts getting too far away from it, it will do a faster charge to reach the player and then slam the ground when its close enough.

The Bearger's 'aggro' range is quite small, so the player can get quite close to it without making it aggressive. While idle, the Bearger will eat any food off the ground and attack nearby mobs. If the player picks up a piece of food he was about to eat, he will let out an angry growl, and after the third time his food is stolen he will attack the player. While walking, the Bearger destroys trees in its path. It will also throw everything out of Chests and other containers and eat any food inside them. When there is no food in the area, the Bearger will passively follow the player.

When killed, it drops 8 Meat and 1 Thick Fur, which can be used to craft the Hibearnation Vest and the Insulated Pack.

The Bearger is also attracted to Bee boxes, however fighting it near honey farms is not recommended, for it does not choose honey over the player when aggroed.

Strategy

The bearger will use swipe when it gets close to the player, and knock your weapon out of your hand.

The swipe will deal damage in the same range as the Beargers melee range:

Once the Bearger has used 3 swipes or had to chase the player, it will then use a slam that also knocks your weapon out of your hand and destroys objects around it:

The slam also have the same damage range as his melee range:

You will have enough time to attack him 2-3 times before having to dodge, except after he used his slam, as a swipe comes right after.

It is recommended bringing at least a Log Suit and a Football Helmet to the fight, or something better. Be careful when fighting near an ocean as weapons can get knocked into the water.

One of the fastest ways to deal with any boss in Don't Starve is a Sleep Dart/Pan Flute and Old Bell/14 Gunpowder combination. Not only does the 14 Gunpowder bring the Bearger down to 200 health, but you can easily take it down from there with 2 Blow Darts and still get its loot. The Old Bell on the other hand, can be used thrice to finish it off while still obtaining its loot. While it may seem easy to take it down, it is hard to obtain these items. For example, the Old Bell requires the Glommer to be found and killed. Another way to easily kill one is to lure it into a forest and let it destroy the Trees until a Treeguard spawns.
