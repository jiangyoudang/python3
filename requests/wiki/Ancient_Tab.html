The Ancient Tab allows the players to craft objects using the Ancient Pseudoscience Station. Items under this tab cannot be "prototyped" and then made on the go in the future; every time an item under this tab is crafted, the player must be near an ancient pseudoscience station, no matter how many times the player has crafted the same item before.
