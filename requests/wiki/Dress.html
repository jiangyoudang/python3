Dress items can be obtained from Crafting, in the Dress tab with the Teal Top Hat icon on the left-hand side. Many Dress items have durability, which is consumed over time by simply wearing them. A Sewing Kit can be used to restore a Dress item's durability, unless it is fully depleted, in which case the item is destroyed. The Garland is the only Dress item available at the start of the game and does not have durability, instead spoiling over time like food.

Some mobs can wear hats, either those given to them or stolen from the player. These include Pigs (and Werepigs), Guardian Pigs, Bunnymen (and Beardlords), and Splumonkeys. Killing the mobs will drop the hat, though the player can swap hats on Pigs and Bunnymen.
