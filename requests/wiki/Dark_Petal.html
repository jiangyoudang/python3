Petals are a Vegetable Food item obtained by picking Flowers, which are commonly found in Grassland biomes, or harvesting Tumbleweed. They restore 5 Sanity if picked and 1 health point when eaten. Like other vegetables, they can be fed to Pigs to produce Manure.

Petals can be used to craft a Garland, a headwear item which restores Sanity over time.

In addition to a Garland, petals can be used to create a Pretty Parasol and prototype a Floral Shirt In the Reign of Giants DLC.

Dark Petals

Dark Petals are obtained by picking Evil Flowers that spawn around the Things, Maxwell's Door or a Totally Normal Tree. Evil Flowers drain the player's Sanity by 5 when picked, the opposite of normal flowers. Dark Petals can be eaten to drain an additional 5 Sanity points or crafted into Nightmare Fuel using a Shadow Manipulator or a Prestihatitator.

Hiveless bees are able to pollinate and create new Evil Flowers, also Tumbleweed can drop all types of petals when harvested, making Dark Petals a renewable resource.

Foliage

Foliage is a vegetable food item obtained by picking a Fern or harvesting Tumbleweed. Similar to Petals, Foliage can be eaten to restore 1 Health or used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit. They can be crafted into a Potted Fern.
