Shop Keeper

DebugSpawn "basic_shop"

The Shopkeeper is an unimplemented feature. The lead developer, Kevin, has stated a few times, that he "hates that guy" and that he will never be implemented.[citation needed] The Shop Keeper is only available from spawning it through the in-game lua console.

Clicking on the Shop Keeper will result in crashing your game, so click with discretion.

Trivia

  • In the game files, there are more shops, which could mean that there used to be separate shops that sold different things.
