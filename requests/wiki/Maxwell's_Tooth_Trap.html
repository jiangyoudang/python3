Maxwell's Tooth Trap

Perk 60 damage to player when activated
Spawn Code "trap_teeth_maxwell"
“ A crude attempt at tricking me. ”

–Wickerbottom

Maxwell's Tooth Trap is an object found only in Adventure Mode. It is similar to a normal Tooth Trap, though it will trigger if the player and any other mobs step on it, dealing 60 damage.

Maxwell's Tooth Trap can't be picked up, and it will disappear once activated.

Maxwell's Tooth Traps were once easily avoidable. However, recent updates have changed the way that these function. As opposed to previous versions where they were visible they now lie buried just beneath the surface, ready for an unfortunate fool to accidentally step on them. The number of existing traps in a field is now nearly impossible to tell.

Strategy

It is better to lead some friendly Pigs to the Maxwell's Tooth Trap, as the player can eliminate the tooth trap and get some loot from the Pig at the same time. Leading any aggressive mob for their loot is also an option.

Previously, it was difficult to lead Chester through such minefields. However, the current Chester model cannot activate traps, making him much less likely to die.
