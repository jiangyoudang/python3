Papyrus is a Refined item crafted with 4 Cut Reeds. A Science Machine is required to unlock the recipe. Papyrus can be used to make Honey Poultice for healing as well as a Birdcage, Night Armor, and Compass. Wickerbottom can use it to craft Books and starts out with two Papyrus.
