Glommer's Wings are a nonperishable item dropped by Glommers. It is used to craft the Old Bell. It is renewable, as the player can obtain one each Full Moon by killing Glommer.

The Wings can also be used as fuel for fires, or the Ice Flingomatic in case the player doesn't have a Glommer's Flower to make the Old Bell.
