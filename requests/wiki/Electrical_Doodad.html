An Electrical Doodad is a Science item from the Reign of Giants DLC. It is an intermediate-step crafting product used in many different recipes and is crafted using 1 Cut Stone and 2 Gold Nuggets.near a Science Machine. In addition to that Electrical Doodads have a small chance to be vomited up by friendly Catcoons.
