The Abyss acts as a barrier, much like the Ocean. It is only present in Caves and Ruins in Sandbox Mode, and in Chapter 6 of Adventure Mode. No living thing can cross the abyss, even flying mobs like Batilisks.

Objects that are situated very close to the abyss may drop their items in this endless pit, and aside from bugged objects, they will fall into the depths and be unretrievable.

Bugs

  • Items that drop in the abyss will make a splash animation as if it were made of water.
