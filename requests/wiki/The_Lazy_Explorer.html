The Lazy Explorer is a staff used for teleporting across the screen at the cost of 15 Sanity. When equipped, a prompt to "Telepoof" shows up when the cursor is on the field, and right-clicking will almost immediately teleport the character to that spot.

Telepoofing will not work on spots where the cursor is unavailable, such as the Ocean or in the dark.

The Lazy Explorer also increases movement speed by 25% while equipped, the same as the Walking Cane. It can also be used as a light weapon as the Walking Cane can, but only if the player forces an attack. Attacking with it does 10 damage and doesn't cause it to lose durability.

Trivia

  • One can stack the speed bonus with the Magiluminescence to have a total increase of 50% movement speed while both items are worn, which means that while on the road, the player will have a terrific 100% speed boost.
  • When teleporting across gaps, any followers you had at the moment (including Chester) will teleport off-screen near your location.
  • Wendy's quote is a reference to the idea that a real life teleportation would not work as it does in the movies or games. In the real world, scientists speculate, a teleporter would destroy (by taking them apart at the atomic level) the subject at the first location, effectivly killing them, and then create an exact copy at the new location.

Bugs

  • When teleporting across gaps, it may cause the player to be off the map.
  • Occasionally, if the player teleports too quickly, the sound effect of swinging will be played, however the rod will not swing, and the player will also not teleport. This can be fixed by walking a short distance
