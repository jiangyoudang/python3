Maxwell's Mosquito Trap

DebugSpawn "beemine_maxwell"
“ Whoever put that is a bad fella. ”

–Wolfgang

Maxwell's Mosquito Trap is an upcoming/unimplemented item that acts in the same way as the Bee Mine, but it spawns Mosquitoes instead of Bees. It will be activated when the character or another mob steps on it, causing the spawned Mosquitoes to attack them. Maxwell's Mosquito Trap cannot be picked up. When Wilson examines it, he says, "Bottled mosquito rage!"

It is similar to Maxwell's Tooth Trap: it can't be picked up, and it only emerges in Adventure Mode.
