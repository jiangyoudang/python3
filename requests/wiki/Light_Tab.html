“ Sure beats darkness. ”

–Wilson

Light is used to illuminate the area at Night, lowering that rate at which Sanity decreases and protecting against the Night Monster, who will attack in total darkness.

Active light sources will also prevent friendly or homeless pigs from panicking, and lets them stay/sleep near its light.
