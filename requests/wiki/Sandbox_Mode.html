Sandbox Mode is the game mode in which players begin their world. Prior to starting a new game, the player has the option to customize their world and may set things such as the frequency of Hound attacks or the occurrence of seasons. When a new world is started, the player is set in a randomly-generated world and is told by Maxwell to find some food before night comes.

In order to enter Adventure Mode, the player must find Maxwell's Door, which is located somewhere in the world, though usually quite far from where the player first spawned.

    Sandbox (Custom)

    There are a total of 30 customization options available when creating a new world. These customization options serve to allow the player to tweak a world to their liking. Note that setting many of the options that concern spawn rates to anything above the "default" value will cause those resources to spawn outside of their native biomes. Examples include Reeds and Beefalos. The options are shown below:

    Type Content
    Carrots Determine the amount of naturally spawning carrots. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Berry Bushes Determine the amount of naturally spawning Berry Bushes. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Mandrakes Determine the amount of naturally spawning Mandrakes. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Land Branching Determine the degree to which the land will branch off. Available options are least, default and most.
    Touch Stones Determine the amount of Touchstones that spawn on the map. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    World Size Determine the size of the world. Available options are default, medium, large, and huge.
    Seasons Influence the behaviour of Summer and Winter. Available options are only Summer, only Winter, Default, long Summer, long Winter, long both and short both.
    Lightning Determine the frequency of lightning strikes. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Day Determine the length and/or presence/absence of Day, Dusk, and Night. Available options are only day, only dusk, only night, default, long day, long dusk and long night.
    Boons Determine the amount of spawned Set Pieces.  Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Weather Set the frequency of weather events. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Season Start Select the starting season. Available options are Summer and Winter.
    Land Loop Determine the degree to which the roads will tend to lead you in circles, in addition to determine the degree to which the land will curve. Available options are none, default and always.
    Beefalo Determine the population of Beefalo. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Perd Determine the frequency of spawning Gobblers. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Tallbirds Determine the population of Tallbird Nests. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Rabbits Determine the population of Rabbit Holes. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Bees Determine the population of Beehives. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Pigs Determine the population of Pig Houses. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Beefaloheat Determine the frequency at which Beefalo will go into heat. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Frogs Determine the population of Ponds. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Tentacles Determine the population of Tentacles. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Hounds Determine the frequency of Hound attacks. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Treeguards Determine the spawn rate of Treeguards. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Krampus Determine the spawn rate of Krampus. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Deerclops Determine the spawn rate of Deerclops. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Grass Determine the amount of naturally spawning Grass. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Rocks Determine the amount of naturally spawning Rocks. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Reeds Determine the amount of naturally spawning Reeds. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Saplings Determine the amount of naturally spawning Saplings. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.
    Trees Determine the amount of naturally spawning Trees. Available options are none, less, default, more and lots.

    Additional information concerning specific consequences of the customization options:

    • A long season lasts ~50 days.
    • A short season lasts ~10 days.
    • A long day is 14 daylight hours, 1 dusk hour and 1 night hour of the clock.
    • A long dusk is 7 daylight hours, 6 dusk hours and 3 night hours of the clock.
    • A long night is 5 daylight hours, 2 dusk hours and 9 night hours of the clock.
    • Resources, animals, and foods set to lots will spawn in every biome, with the exception of plants in Rockyland.
    • Rain set to lots is infinite.
    • When Lightning is set to lots, lightning will strike every 12–30 seconds.
    • More information on the frequency of Hound attacks can be found here.

    Default Plus

    At the start of the game, it spawns you with food and many useful items to quickly establish a base. However, resources around the map are scarcer and monsters are more numerous. Boons are also more common than in Default.

    Trivia

    • Although they are valid monsters in-game, the customization interface does not allow the player to tweak the appearance of Mosquitoes.  
    • Setting objects to "None", will not prevent them spawning in set pieces, such as trees around Maxwell's Door.

    Bugs

    • If your character spawns with monsters near you, they can attack you before Maxwell finishes his intro speech. (Verified with Tallbirds.) Clicking will allow you to skip Maxwell's speech and react accordingly.
