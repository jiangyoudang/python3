The World Generation screen is the Transition/Loading phase between choosing a character and playing, these show a pair of dark skinny hands residing over a small globe that has little 2-d models of most of the In game items or objects. They also have little phrases such as "Wrangling monsters" and "Insulating Wilson's Beard" which are generally in-game related.

    Phrases

    The phrases printed in the loading screen are a combination of two phrases put together. The possible phrases are listed below.

    First word/Phrase part:

    • "Insulating"
    • "Wrangling"
    • "Inserting"
    • "Reticulating"
    • "Embiggining"
    • "Insinuating"
    • "Framing"

    Second Word/Phrase part:

    • "Wilson's Beard"
    • "Monsters"
    • "Spiders"
    • "Buckets and Buckets of loot"
    • "Food"
    • "Pines"
    • "Pathos"
    • "A keen sense of depair"
    • "Game Theory"

    Caves

    When you enter a Caves, there is now a different, unique World Generation screen. The sphere is rocky instead of grassy or dirtlike, and you can see the many structures and animals of the Caves, such as Bunnymen, Stalagmites and Mushtrees.
