The Harp Statue is a structure that can be mined with a Pickaxe for 2-3 Marble. It looks like a headless angel with a harp in its hands, though there is a head included in the text files.

Players may find a Harp Statue around the Wooden Thing in Sandbox Mode and easily in the Adventure Mode world, The Game is Afoot, as there is a large Chess Biome there.
