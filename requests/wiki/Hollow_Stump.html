The Hollow Stump is an object found in the Reign of Giants DLC.  It has yarn stuck to its sticks and a yarn ball on the side. It is the home of the Catcoon, which respawns 8 times from it after death (for a total of 9 lives) each 4 days. It can be hammered to yield 2 Twigs, 2 Logs, and a chance of another random item.

After the Catcoon living in it has run out of lives, the Hollow Stump will go into a "dead" state and will serve no other purpose other than being hammered down.
