For the time being the door only exists in Don't Starve's Game Files.

You may find it here: 

C:/Program Files (x86)/Steam/SteamApps/common/don't starve/data/anim/door.zip

Since it exists in the game files the door will most likely be added in one of the future Updates.

It would probably be the first possibility to connect walls with each other without making passing through impossible. There might be various ways in which a door could be added to the game.

For example doors could be unbreakable and Mobs that would attack you while you are in your fortress (such as Hounds) might be idling before your door and wait for you to come out; and sooner or later you'd sure have to. A second way for mobs to get rid of the problem could be that they would be able to destroy the door. The door would probably be the weakest element of your walls and therefore the target of highest prority for the mobs.

But since it has not been added yet, everything written in the paragraph above is entirely Speculation.
