The Glommer's Statue is a statue object that appears in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. It drops three Marble and a blueprint for the Old Bell. On a full moon, a Glommer will appear and a flower will spawn on the statue. It spawns in the Deciduous Forest biome. After mining the statue, the base will remain, allowing a Glommer and flower to respawn at a full moon.
