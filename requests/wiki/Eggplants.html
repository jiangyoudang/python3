Eggplant is an uncommon Vegetable that can be farmed from Basic Farms and Improved Farms by planting Seeds for a 11% chance of growing or using Eggplant Seeds. It can be used as an ingredient in several Crock Pot recipes. Feeding an Eggplant to a bird in a Birdcage will produce 1–2 Eggplant Seeds and half the time 1 regular bunch of Seeds.

Braised Eggplant

Braised Eggplant is a Vegetable Food item made by Cooking an Eggplant on fire (Campfire, Fire Pit, or Star). It restores much more health than its raw counterpart. It can also be used as an ingredient in several Crock Pot recipes.
