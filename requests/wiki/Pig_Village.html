A Pig Village is a settlement of Pigs and 5-9 Pig Houses, sometimes with or without a Pig King in it who's surrounded by Obelisks. Sometimes there are Carrots, Flowers, Grass Tufts, and Berry Bushes in farms of nine, fewer if the amount of those plants (other than Flowers) has been tweaked for Sandbox Mode. Some villages may also spawn with Merm Heads.

Pig Villages usually spawn in a Grassland biome close to a Forest biome, though they rarely spawn in other biomes such as Rockyland or Savanna. A Cobblestone Road connects two Pig Villages but more Pig Villages can be found rarely by following a regular dirt road.

Other Associated Structures

  • In the Caves, there is a special type of house (Rabbit Hutch) that Bunnymen live in.
  • In Marsh biome, the Pig Houses and the Pigs are replaced with Rundown Houses and Merms.
