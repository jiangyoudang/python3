Stalagmite Biomes are an environment that can only be found inside a cave. It has been named after the abundance of Stalagmites that can be found in the area. Turfs like Cave Rock Turf and Guano Turfs are also abundant and covers the entire area. There are no known plants that can be found in these biomes. There are two types of Stalagmite Biomes each have different features:

The first one is being abundant with normal stalagmites. These areas are known to be infested with Spitters making it their natural habitat. Although there is also a branch of this biome which is not infested depending on the world generation. Players are advised to take caution when venturing in this area. The second one is being abundant with tall stalagmites and far less dangerous than the first one. These are the places where a Batilisks, Guanos, and Nightmare Fissures can be rarely found. Even if Batilisks are far less dangerous than the Spitters they still possess a threat to a player.
