The Sewing Kit is a Dress item that allows the player to restore lost durability on all clothing items.

In order to use the Sewing Kit, the player must select the Kit from their inventory and then right-click the article of clothing they wish to repair. Alternatively, the player may repair an article of clothing that is resting on the ground. The Sewing Kit has 5 uses before it breaks. One use restores 5 days of durability to an item.

Since typically Hounds appear roughly every week, and after the first few weeks will usually come in enough numbers to replace stock, rare items of clothing can be regularly worn without much concern for them degrading entirely.

It should be noted that all articles of clothing will not lose their durability when they are in an inventory or a container, only when the player wears them.

Trivia

  • The Sewing Kit was added in The End of the Beginning Update.
  • Before the Underground update, clothing had to be removed to be repaired by the Sewing Kit.
  • When Wilson examines a Sewing Kit, he will say, "Darn it! Darn it all to heck!" This is a pun, as "darning" is a sewing technique for repairing holes in damaged clothing.
