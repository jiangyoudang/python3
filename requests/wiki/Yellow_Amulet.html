Magiluminescence is an Ancient Amulet that emits light. The player also gets a Sanity boost of +2/min and a speed boost of 25%.

While wearing it, it will provide a small amount of light around the player with the radius of a Torch and grants the player more running speed like the Walking Cane. Basically, this amulet is a combination of the Torch and the Walking Cane, so it is good for both exploring in the night or in the Caves and running away from Monsters. It has Durability and degrades over time while worn.

Trivia

  • When placed on the ground, the Magiluminescence used to glow and not lose any durability. As of the A Moderately Friendly Update, they will no longer glow when dropped on the ground.
  • You can stack the speed bonus with the Walking Cane to have a total increase of 50% movement speed while both items are worn. This means that while on the road you will have a terrific 100% speed boost.
