–Willow

Ash is an Item that replaces any item that cannot be taken to the next World by using the Wooden Thing or any item that has been burnt, including the remnants of a Campfire. Mobs that are killed by fire will drop the cooked version of their meat and Ash in place of other items such as Living Logs. Fire Hounds drop 3 pieces of Ash upon death as well.

Ash is an ingredient for crafting Healing Salve.

Ash is blown away by the wind after 20 seconds on the ground. Picking it up and then dropping it again resets the timer.
