This page is about different kinds of Meats. For the item itself, see Meat.

There are 11 basic Meat food items in-game, some of them with respective cooked and dried versions. Most meats are dropped by Mobs as loot, and fish can also be obtained from Ponds with a Fishing Rod. If a mob died from burning, cooked variant of meat will be dropped instead (if available). Mobs, such as Pigs, Spiders or Hounds, will eat any form of dropped meat on the ground, including Pig Skins.

Additionally, Bunnymen will be aggressive towards a player if they have any item on this list.

Some of meats can be used in Crock Pot recipes. Egg is counted as an egg in a Crock Pot, and as meat elsewhere. Fish is only counted as fish in a Crock Pot, and as meat elsewhere.

Usage

  • There are 13 Crock Pot recipes that utilize meat.
  • Meats except for raw in any state of spoilage can be given to the bird in a Birdcage to produce a fresh .
  • Meats can be given to Pigs to befriend them. If the Pig eats four of any of the health damaging monster foods, it will turn into a Werepig.
  • Meats except for can be given to the Pig King in exchange for Gold Nuggets
