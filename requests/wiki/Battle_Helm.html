The Battle Helm a piece of Armor in the Reign of Giants exclusive to Wigfrid. It resembles a studded leather helmet with a winged unicorn adornment. Wigfrid starts the game with it in her inventory and is the only character able to craft it. It absorbs 80% of incoming damage and provides a 20% bonus to water resistance.

Due its stats and cheap recipe its a great alternative to the Football Helmet and will secure the head armor place for a long time for Wigfrid players.
