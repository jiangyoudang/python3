Creature Hostility Matrix - Creatures may have different reactions based on player actions and situation

  • Hostile creatures will attack when conditions are met (normally when entering range, but not always)
  • Neutral creatures will defend themselves, but will not initiate an attack
  • Passive creatures will not defend or attack, but may run away
  • Assist indicates creatures that may follow the player and try to assist, depending on situation
  • Bosses generally have much more health and are more dangerous
  • Pure is an Innocent creature ("Pure" used for formatting) - killing one causes karma loss, which leads to spawning Krampus
  • Notes are abbreviated summaries—see the creature's page for details
