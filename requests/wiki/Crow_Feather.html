“ IT HAS BEEN SEPARATED FROM ITS BIRD. ”

–WX-78

There are three types of bird feathers in Don't Starve: the Crimson Feather, the Jet Feather and the Azure Feather. They can be obtained by murdering captive birds, or by killing them in action or on the ground. Each bird has a 50% chance of dropping its feather, whereas Pengulls only have a 10% chance of dropping one (along with fairly good odds of dropping absolutely nothing).

Crimson Feather

The Crimson Feather is dropped by Redbirds, Crimson Feathers can be used to make Fire Darts and Feather Hats.


Crimson Feather Recipes 

Jet Feather

The Jet Feather is dropped by Crows and Pengulls, Jet Feathers can be used to make Sleep Darts and Feather Hats.


Jet Feather Recipes 

Azure Feather

The Azure Feather is dropped by Snowbirds, Azure feathers can be used to make Blowdarts. Snowbirds only appear during Winter.

Azure Feather Recipes

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, all 3 of the feathers can be found by destroying Tumbleweed or thrown up by a Catcoon.

They can also be used as fuel.

Trivia

  • Before the "Strange new Powers" update, Crimson Feathers were called Crimson Feathers, Jet Feathers were called Crow Feathers, and Azure Feathers were called Snowbird Feathers.
