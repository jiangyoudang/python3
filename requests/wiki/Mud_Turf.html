Mud Turf

Fuel Value 90/45 sec
Dropped by Mud Turf tiles (Pitchfork)
Stacks up to 10
DebugSpawn "turf_mud"


Mud Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging Mud Turfs tiles with a Pitchfork and can only be found in a Cave . It is one of the two turf that connects or surround the Sunken Forest. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or a Fire Pit.
