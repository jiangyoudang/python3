The Moon Cycle is a gameplay mechanic which controls the appearance of Werepigs and Woodie's transformation. Werepigs are normal Pigs who are caught outdoors during a full moon, and Woodie turns into a Werebeaver whenever there's a full moon (or he chops too many trees).

During a full moon, Chester will transform into either a Shadow Chester if he has Nightmare Fuel in every inventory slot or a Snow Chester if he has Blue Gems in every slot.

The complete Moon Cycle lasts for 16 days and then repeats. Each phase of the Moon lasts for two nights.

Day(s) Moon Phase
 1 - 2 New Moon
 3 - 4 Quarter (Crescent) Moon
 5 - 6 Half Moon
 7 - 8 3/4 (Gibbous) Moon
 9 - 10 Full Moon
11 - 12 3/4 (Gibbous) Moon
13 - 14 Half Moon
15 - 16 Quarter (Crescent) Moon
17 - 18 New Moon

You can use Lunar Calendar Generator to see past day 18 if you're too lazy to simply follow the pattern.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, several new things can happen on a full moon. The moon now casts down a dim, blue-tinted light over the world, so the player can safely explore without fear of Charlie. Mushrooms have a chance of turning into Mushtrees. Flowers can turn into Evil Flower. The eyes on Pig and Merm heads will glow white, drop Nightmare Fuel when hammered, and generate an insanity aura. Ghosts will emerge from any type of Grave. Glommer's Statue also grows Glommer's Flower, and causes a Glommer to spawn nearby.

Full Moon can also affect the Cave worlds. Unlike in the surface, Full Moon does not provide light visibility to this world since caves are unground. Therefore, characters are required to have sufficient light source while spelunking. Just like in the surface, the screen tone will change into bluish color much like in Winter. Mushrooms also have a chance of turning into Mushtrees, the same goes to Pigs and Flowers which are located in a cave.

Trivia

  • By default, it takes 560 days for the beginning of the Moon Cycle to re-align with the first day of the starting season.
  • When both seasons are set to "Long", 12 complete moon cycles will occur in 4 seasons time (similar to a standard calendar year).
