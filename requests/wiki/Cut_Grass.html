Cut Grass

Fuel Value 30/15 sec
Dropped by Harvesting Grass Tufts, (40% x3)
Stacks up to 40
DebugSpawn "cutgrass"
“ Cut grass, ready for arts and crafts. ”

–Wilson

Cut Grass is a renewable resource item harvested from Grass Tufts found naturally in the world. It can be used on numerous starter recipes and in even more advanced items when refined into Rope.

Reign of Giants

In Reign of Giants, Cut Grass now drops from all Tumbleweeds, making it renewable even if the player burns all the spawned Grass Tufts.

Cut Grass can also be gained from crops on a Farm that are destroyed by the heat during summer.
