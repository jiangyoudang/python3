–Wolfgang

The Endothermic Fire is a craftable Light Tab structure found in the Reign of Giants DLC. It serves as a counterpart to the Campfire, the differences being that instead of warming the player, it cools them down, and it is unable to cook food. It was created to help the player deal with the new overheating mechanic. When burnt out, it drops Ash.

Contents

[show]

    Endothermic Fire Pit

    The Endothermic Fire Pit serves as the permanent version of the Endothermic Fire, and cooling counterpart of the Fire Pit. Endothermic Fires can be fueled by Logs, Twigs, and other materials that can be used on normal fire. In addition, it can also be fueled with Nitre.

    Tips

    • Endothermic Fires are useful in Summer if the player doesn't plan to go far from base, but if travelling, using an Ice Cube or other cooling items is better.

    Trivia

    • Despite not being a real fire, Willow will still regain sanity when standing near it.
