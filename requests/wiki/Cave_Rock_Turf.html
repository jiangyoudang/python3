Cave Rock Turf

Fuel Value 90/45 sec
Dropped by Cave Rock tiles (Pitchfork)
Stacks up to 10
DebugSpawn "turf_underrock"
“ Rocks, rocks, rocks, rocks... ”

–Wilson

Cave Rock Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging Cave Rock Turf tiles with a Pitchfork.  Similar in appearance to the Rocky Turf, the Cave Rock Turf is often found near large numbers of mineable Stalagmites. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit. Additionally like the Rocky Turf, this kind of turf doesn't allow plants to grow on it, including Eyeplants.
