Graves or Mounds are naturally-appearing objects in the world that can be found singularly in forests or in a large group in a graveyard (which is characterized by a large shroud of fog). They may be found with or without a nearby Headstone, though graves without headstones can only be found in actual graveyards.

Each grave dug with a Shovel or Regal Shovel has a 10% chance to spawn a Ghost, which will be aggressive towards the player. Otherwise, the player will uncover an Amulet, Gravedigger Item, Blue Gem, Red Gem, Gears, or Nightmare Fuel. Once dug, the grave will not respawn. Each world has a limited number of them.

Digging a grave takes 4% of the Shovel's durability and 1% of a Regal Shovel's. Each grave dug also causes the player to lose 10 points of Sanity.

Headstone

Headstones are objects that usually spawn next to a Grave. They currently have no usage, except for decoration, and they can not be moved by using a Hammer.

List of possible epitaphs:

  • "This headstone is blank"
  • "It says: 'Milk. Eggs. Bacon.'"
  • "Hey that's my name!"
