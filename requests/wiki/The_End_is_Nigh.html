The Books Tab is a special tab that is only available to Wickerbottom. It allows the player to craft Books, which can be used to activate several events at the cost of sanity. Each book has multiple uses.

Books

Birds of the World

Covers 1000 Species: Habits, habitats, and calls.

  • Call 20 to 30 birds.
  • 3 uses.
  • Costs 50 sanity per use.
  • DebugSpawn: "book_birds"




Recipe

Tier: Always Available

Applied Horticulture

On the care and feeding of plants.

  • Grow crops, plants, and trees instantly in the summer. Does not work on unfertilized crops or plants.
  • 5 uses and 30 range.
  • Costs 33 sanity per use.
  • DebugSpawn: "book_gardening"




Recipe

Tier: Always Available

Sleepytime Stories

Soothing tales to send you off to dreamland.

  • Cause animals to sleep.
  • 5 uses and 30 range.
  • Costs 33 sanity per use.
  • DebugSpawn: "book_sleep"

Let's learn about our subterranean friends!

  • Create 3 tentacles.
  • 5 uses and 3 to 8 range.

Trivia

  • "On Tentacles" is the only recipe that requires a Alchemy Engine to be crafted whilst playing as Wickerbottom, while "The End is Nigh" is the only one that needs a Shadow Manipulator.
  • The phrase Ia! Ia! in the "The End is Nigh" description refers to the phrase "Iä! Iä! Cthulhu fhtagn!", a chant from the Cthulhu mythos of H.P. Lovecraft.
  • The sanity reduction from reading suggests that these books contain knowledge that humans were not meant to know, similar to the Necronomicon and Pnakotic Manuscripts from the writings of H.P. Lovecraft.
