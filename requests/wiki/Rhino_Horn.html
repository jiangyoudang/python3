The Guardian's Horn is an item dropped by Ancient Guardian, which is found in the Ruins underground. The horn can be used to craft a Houndius Shootius.

It is similar to the Deerclops Eyeball: it doesn't spoil, drains the same amount of sanity when eaten, restores the same amount of health and hunger when eaten, and is dropped by a boss mob.

As a meat item, Pigs and Spiders will try to eat a Guardian's Horn, and Bunnymen will be hostile to players carrying it in their inventory.
