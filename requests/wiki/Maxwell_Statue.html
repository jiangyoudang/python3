The Maxwell Statue is a structure that can emerge in all worlds of Adventure Mode and occasionally in Sandbox Mode. It can be mined with a Pickaxe, Opulent Pickaxe or Pick/Axe to obtain Marble. It is used by Maxwell to lock Wes in an invisible chamber.

If a player mines the Maxwell Statue in Epilogue: Checkmate of Adventure Mode, 3-4 Clockwork Monsters will spawn around the statue, and the player's sanity level will drop to 0 immediately. The player must kill all the mobs to restore sanity. However, if the player uses a console command to spawn a Maxwell Statue or finds a statue elsewhere, there will be no special effect on the player upon mining it.

In rare cases, a Maxwell Statue may be present in Pig Fortresses instead of the Pig King, heavily guarded.


Trivia

  • This structure was added on the A Little Rain Must Fall Update, but only naturally generated in the Doorway to Adventure Update.
