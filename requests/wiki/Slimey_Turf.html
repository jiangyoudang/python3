Slimey Turf

Fuel Value 90/45 sec
Dropped by Slimey Turf tiles (Pitchfork)
Stacks up to 10
DebugSpawn "turf_sinkhole"

Slimey Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging Slimey tiles with a Pitchfork and can only be found in Cave . It is one of the two turf that connects or surround a Sunken Forest . Sometimes, a player may also find Saplings in a ground that is covered with Slimey Turf. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.
