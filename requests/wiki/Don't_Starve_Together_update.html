Don't Starve Together is an upcoming expansion which will add the highly requested feature of multiplayer to Don't Starve. It is scheduled to come out in Summer 2014,[1] though a specific date has not been given.

The expansion will be free, but players who wish to buy the game after the expansion has been released will need to pay more; increasing the total cost of the game to $20.

Trivia

  • The way Wilson creates the multiplayer portal is similar to how he created Maxwell's Door.
  • You can see the sillohuettes of what is thought to be Wigfrid, Wes and Wolfgang in the portal.
