Weapons are in-game Items that can be used to fight enemies. They have a durability which decreases with every hit. Wes and Wendy deal 0.75 times normal damage, while Wolfgang's damage varies from 0.5 to 2 times. Unarmed attacks do 10 damage per hit.

    Melee Weapons

    Item Dmg Description
    68 The Dark Sword is the strongest melee weapon in the game and deals 68 damage per hit. It has 100 uses and drains 20 sanity per minute while wielding it.
    		59 The Thulecite Club is the second strongest melee weapon in the game. It has 150 uses and deals 59 damage, and boosts walking speed by 10% when equipped. When using it there is a 20% chance that it will spawn a Shadow Tentacle.

    Ranged Weapons

    Item Dmg Description
    27.2 The Boomerang is a ranged weapon that deals 27.2 damage per hit. Once it has hit an enemy, it will fly back towards the player. Pressing 'Space' while it's on its way back will let the player catch it. If not caught properly, it will damage the player instead, as well as consuming a second use. It has 10 uses.
    100 The Blow Dart will do 100 damage to enemies when shot. Each shot will consume 1 dart.
    The Sleep Dart will put most mobs to sleep after being shot with 1-4 darts. Each shot will consume 1 dart.
    The Fire Dart will set enemies on fire, causing them to panic and set fire to nearby objects. Each shot will consume 1 dart.
    The Ice Staff is a Magic ranged weapon that temporarily freezes an animal or monster after 1-4 blasts. It drains 1 sanity with every use. It has 20 uses.
    The Fire Staff is a Magic ranged weapon that will set enemies on fire. It can also set things on fire directly just like the Torch. Like the Ice Staff, it drains 1 sanity with every use. It has 20 uses.

    Traps

    Item Dmg Description
    60 The Tooth Trap is a placeable trap that will trigger once an enemy is in its proximity, dealing 60 damage per hit. After each use, it will need to be reset by right clicking it. It has 10 uses.
    The Bee Mine is a placeable trap that will trigger once an enemy is in its proximity, releasing 4 bees that target only the enemy that triggered it. Bees do 10 damage,making 40 damage along with the 3 other bees. When the bees' target is killed, they disperse into four different directions.
    The Bird Trap is a placeable, non-lethal trap that can catch Redbirds, Crows, and Snowbirds. Baiting with Seeds increases the chance a bird will land on it. It has 8 uses.
    The Trap is a placeable, non-lethal trap that can catch small animals like Rabbits, Frogs, and Spiders. Placing a bait in it lures nearby animals to go towards the trap. It has 8 uses.

    Explosive weapons

    Item Dmg Description
    200 Gunpowder must be placed near an object and lit on fire. It deals 200 damage after a two to four second delay and destroys objects in the immediate area. Players are advised to leave the area before detonation.
    50 Slurtle Slime must be placed near an object and lit on fire. After four seconds it then explodes, doing 50 damage per stacked slime.
