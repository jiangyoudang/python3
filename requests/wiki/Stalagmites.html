Stalagmites are the underground equivalent of Boulders. They can be mined using either a Pickaxe, Opulent Pickaxe or Pick/Axe. Stalagmites drop various minerals randomly when mined.

Both varieties can be found in 3 different starting states: full, medium, and low. Each type has a different loot table. Even though a stalagmite will appear to reduce to lower states as it is mined, it will retain the loot table of its starting state.

Trivia

  • Confusion often arises, but Stalagmites grow from the floor; Stalactites grow from the ceiling. An easy way to remember this is to remember that Stalactites hang down from the ceiling, while stalagmites grow up from the ground.
  • The fact that tall stalagmites can drop a log suggests they may be petrified trees.
