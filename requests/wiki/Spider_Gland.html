A Spider Gland is a healing item dropped 25% of the time when a Spider is killed or a Spilagmite is mined. When used, this item restores 8 Health to players or mobs. It can also be used to craft a Healing Salve.

Trivia

  • This item was added in Doorway to Adventure update.
  • Prior to the Moderately Friendly Update, the player could use Spider Glands to repair Walls.
  • Spider Glands are very flammable, so it is not recommended to fight spiders too close to an open fire source, because if the Spider Gland is dropped close to the fire, it will catch on fire and be reduced to ash.
  • Spider Glands can heal almost any mob in the game, even hostile monsters.
