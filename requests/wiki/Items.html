Items are anything in the world of Don't Starve which can be picked up and carried around (as opposed to objects, which are stationary). They can be anything at all and are found by foraging, mining, farming or killing mobs, except for a select few which are character-specific items. Most items have a specific function which makes them unique; food items restore hunger, clothing provides warmth, protection against damage and can even boost Sanity, and other items can be used for crafting.

Below is a large list of items, what they stack to, what their uses are, and how to obtain them. Generally, small items can stack to 40, medium sized items can stack to 20 and large sized items can only stack to 10. For example: Flint => 40, Logs => 20 and Boards=> 10.
