“ NATURE NEVER CEASES TO ANNOY ME. ”

–WX-78

The Tree Clump is an upcoming/unimplemented structure. It can't be moved or destroyed and blocks your way. When Wilson examines it, he says "It's almost like someone is trying to prevent me from going somewhere".

It is, however logical to assume the Tree Clump will never be implemented, as it features the old art-style for Trees, and has not been updated to match.

Trivia

  • There is coding for the Tree Clump in the Screecher Mod.
