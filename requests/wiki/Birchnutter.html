The Birchnutter is a small, Slurper-like monster that is spawned in a group from Poison Birchnut Trees. They attack the player by leaping at them, dealing 5 points of damage per hit. They are spawned at intervals to defend the Poison trees and target whatever the tree was targeting at the time. Their numbers paired with the tree's root make it really hard to cut the monster down. They are easily avoidalbe otherwise though and are easy to kill. Birchnutters drop Twigs or Birchnuts, meaning they are a good way to farm Birchnuts.

Birchnutters, unlike Eyeplants can spawn from any kind of turf. They take one hit from an Ice Staff to be frozen but they can't be put to sleep. Instead, they go back underground when a Pan Flute or other similar items are used against them.
