Relics are the breakable remains of the Ancient Civilization found in the Ruins. When destroyed, they can drop various items, depending on which type of relic was broken.  Plates, bowls, and chip bowls drop nothing. Tables and chairs can drop Rocks, Cut Stone, and Frazzled Wires, with a small chance to drop Gears, Green Gems, Yellow Gems, Orange Gems, or Nightmare Fuel. The vase has a small chance to drop a number of Trinkets, as well as an even smaller chance of spawning Silk, Thulecite, a Sewing Kit, a Cave Spider, a Spitter, or a Splumonkey. They are listed in the prefab file titled "smashables".

Occasionally the player may find broken relics in the Ruins. The player can repair these with Rocks to restore 20 sanity.
