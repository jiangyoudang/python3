Deciduous Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging deciduous tiles with a Pitchfork and can be found covering the entire ground of a Deciduous Forest. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.
