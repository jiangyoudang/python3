The Lost Fragment is a special mod downloaded by clicking the film reel from this page. This mod was a part of one of the William Carter Puzzles that reveal canon information about the character Maxwell.

Upon adding the mod to your Don't Starve folder and enabling it in the main menu, you will be able to craft a special Magic Tab structure, the Shadow Portal.

On activation it will send a "Neat!" or "Nope" prompt, similar to the Maxwell's Door.

Fragmented World

The portal will then send you to a small island, the island is made up of Spiky trees, and parts of a chess biome floor.

Nearby is a Skull Chest that contains a Dark Sword and Night Armor.

Upon opening the chest, you must battle a Shadow Maxwell that is equipped with his own Dark Sword and a special Telelocator Staff that is able to summon high-damage explosive meteors that can easily kill the player in one hit, should they go about unarmored. You should see the shadow of the meteor show where it is about to land, you will be able to dodge it if you keep moving.

Upon defeating waves of shadow Maxwells, the portal will reactivate and interacting with it bring you back to your current world, with all items intact. If you stick around after beating the arena, you can activate the special shadow Phonograph that will spawn. On turning it on, it will play morse code, that when translated, says "exordium". Upon entering the password to this page, you will be redirected to this hidden the hidden video webpage.

Trivia

  • There is a broken bridge leading to a large island. On this island are very few plants that can be typically found in grassland biomes.
  • The crafting quote for the Shadow Portal ("Oh, the places you'll go") is a possible reference to "Oh the Places You'll Go" book by Dr. Seuss.
