Cave Bananas are food items found on Cave Banana Trees. They grow underground and will regrow after 4 days once picked. Cave Bananas can be added to the Crock Pot as Fruit fillers, worth 1 fruit value.

Splumonkeys will pick Cave Bananas from Cave Banana Trees on sight, which has no effect on the 4-day regrowth period of the bananas. The monkeys also are more likely to follow and steal from a player if the player is carrying any number of Cave Bananas.

Cooked Banana

Cooked Bananas are the product of cooking a Cave Banana over a Campfire, Fire Pit, or Star. Like most fruits and vegetables, cooking increases the Health bonus slightly while decreasing the spoil time.
