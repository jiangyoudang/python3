The Gramaphone (or Phonograph) is an object that is seen in the Epilogue of Adventure Mode, nearby Maxwell in the Nightmare Throne. It cannot be destroyed in any way, and it serves no practical function within the game. It will play the Ragtime theme that is used in some update trailers.

The Gramaphone can be turned off and back on. Upon turning it off, Maxwell will say, "Thank you. I have been listening to that song for an eternity." When turned back on, Maxwell will say: "Well, I guess I deserve that". Freeing Maxwell will automatically stop the Gramaphone, but once the player character is in the throne, the Gramaphone will play again, to taunt the player as it did for Maxwell.

Trivia

Fan theories suggest that it's possible to craft the Gramaphone in some way due to the existence of unnamed pieces that look like the components for the device. These pieces can't be acquired without cheating tools or code trickery and serve no purpose for the moment.


Trivia

  • The Gramaphone music emanates from Maxwell's Door while it is open.
  • The game refers to the this device as Gramaphone while its real life counterpart is called Gramophone. Whether it is a mistake or purposeful is not known.
  • If the player spawns the phonograph using the console, and is resurrected next to it via meat effigy, the Gramaphone will be playing.
