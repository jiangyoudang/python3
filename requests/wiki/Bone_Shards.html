Bone Shards are an item found in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. They are obtained by hammering Bones and Skeletons, destroying Tumbleweed, attacking Hound Mounds, or vomited from friendly Catcoons and are used in several crafting recipes.

After all the natural Bones, Skeletons or Hound Mounds in the world have been destroyed, the only way to farm Bone Shards is by killing Big Tentacles or suicide and hammer the skeletons left by the player.
