Road Turf

Fuel Value 45 sec (1.5 segments)
Dropped by Road tiles (Shovel)
Stacks up to 10


Road Turf was one of the Turf items acquired by digging Road tiles with a Pitchfork. Like other Turfs, it could be placed on any bare ground tile. The player's speed was increased when walking on Road Turf. This made Roads a lot easier to construct.

Road Turfs have been replaced by Cobblestones.
