Meat is a Food resource dropped by Pigs, Guardian Pigs, Werepigs, Tallbirds, Smallish Tallbirds, Beefalo, Baby Beefalo (Teen), Koalefant, Rock Lobster, MacTusk, WeeTusk, Deerclops, Ancient Guardian, and Bunnymen. It can be used in multiple Crock Pot Recipes. It also can be dried on a Drying Rack for 2 days to give a Jerky.

See general meat uses.

Cooked Meat

Cooked Meat is Meat that has been cooked on a Campfire, Fire Pit, or Star. It can also be used in multiple recipes. Mobs that ordinarily drop Meat and die from being set on fire will drop Cooked Meat instead. Cooked Meat is required to make Meat Effigies.
