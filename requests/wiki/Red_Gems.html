The Red Gem is an item used to craft several Magical items. It can be combined with a Blue Gem to create a Purple Gem, which used to craft the Shadow Manipulator and other magic items.

Gathering

Red Gems can be obtained from Graves, as a drop from Red Hounds, from Earthquakes and Stalagmites in Caves, and from Ancient Statues and Broken Clockworks in Ruins.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC there is an alternative method of getting Red Gems, by destroying Tumbleweed.
