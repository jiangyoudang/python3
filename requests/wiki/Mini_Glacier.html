“ Rocks made of water. ”

–Wolfgang

Mini Glaciers are naturally occurring objects found in the "Reign of Giants" DLC. When mined, they will drop Ice and Rocks. Mini Glaciers are only at their fullest during the Winter and will begin to melt in the Spring. By Summer, all that will be left is a puddle of water, and the player will have to wait until Autumn for them to slowly begin to freeze again. When mined, Mini Glaciers make a different sound from boulders.

Additionally, Mini Glaciers will appear next to Pengull breeding grounds during Winter, making them a renewable resource.
