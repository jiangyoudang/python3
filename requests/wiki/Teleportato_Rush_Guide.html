Alternative starts

  • You can get Gears for Divining Rod from 3.07% drop (Graves). But you still need resources to build Alchemy Engine.
  • You can go insane and fight Crawling Horrors (9 hits with Spear to kill) and Terrorbeaks (12 hits). Chance to drop Nightmare Fuel is quite good: 150%. This way you will avoid need to build Prestihatitator and other time consuming tasks: farming silk, 4 rabbits + trap(s). But this way didn't help you with Gear anyway.
  • You can rush Ruins both for Gears and Nightmare Fuel

Tips

  • Don't spend time more than you absolutely need (all you need is 4 things and Teleportato in one place, no need for Divining Rod, even it is extremely useful)
  • Divining Rod is the most useful item.
  • Hammer pig houses to get boards and pigs skins (1 for Football Helmet, rest to get pigs as followers)
  • Carrots doesn't have pick up animation, use them as primary food source.
  • Green Mushroom may be useful after you find 16 evil flowers.
  • Try to pick berries only when you Starving, since harvest animation wastes time. Consider picking them only around Pig Torches or in packs of 9. If you have monster meat (from Spiders) to make Meatballs.
