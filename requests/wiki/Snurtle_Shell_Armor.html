Snurtle Shell Armor is Armor worn on the chest that is dropped by Snurtles.

When wearing it normally, it absorbs 60% of all physical damage. When hiding inside the shell (by right-clicking on it in the inventory), it absorbs 100% of all physical damage. Additionally, after hiding inside the shell for around 5 seconds, all attackers will lose interest in the player and stop their attacks.

Trivia

  • It works like a Bush Hat in that aggressive mobs will not attack the player if they hide before they aggro on them. This means that the shell can be used to avoid Hounds attacks and Shadow Creatures.
  • Characters hide head-first in the shell leaving their behind and feet visible.
