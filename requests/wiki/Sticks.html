Twigs are one of the first items acquired at the start of a new default game, along with Grass Tuft, and is needed to craft many tools and other useful items.

Twigs are one of the most needed items in the game and should be, along with Flint, the first thing a player gathers at the start of a new game.

Twigs can be collected from Saplings, Spiky Bushes, Cave Banana Trees and Spiky Trees, all of which are found planted in the ground. They can be harvested and dug up with a Shovel, and Spiky Trees can be chopped using an Axe (or a Luxury Axe). Each Sapling will drop 1 Twig and then become wasted and won't be harvestable until it regrows. Saplings and Spiky Bushes will regrow in 4 days after collecting. Spiky Trees drop 1 Twig and will not regrow. Twigs can sometimes be found as part of random loot from Skeletons (dead adventurers).

Twigs can be used as filler in many Crock Pot recipes and are required for making Fishsticks, Powdercake and Kabobs.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC, Twigs can now be harvested from Tumbleweeds, making them 100% renewable even if all the saplings and spiky bushes end up burning. It can also be used in a new recipe, the Battle Spear.
