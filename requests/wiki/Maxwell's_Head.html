Maxwell's Head

DebugSpawn "maxwellhead"
“ Maxwell might be looking for this. ”

–Wilson

Maxwell's Head is upcoming/unimplemented feature, and can only be seen by being spawned in via the console. Upon being spawned, Maxwell's Head will say, "You forgot to set a speech", and then, "Go do it." He will disappear afterward. He uses Maxwell's speech sound files when he talks.

It is also seen at the end of the "Forbidden Knowledge" trailer.

Editing its speech

It is possible to set the head's "speech" by going into the speech files. First, locate Steam/SteamApps/common/dont_starve/data/scripts/prefabs, then find "maxwellhead" and open it with Notepad. Then, in Notepad, look for text that reads, "string = You forgot to set a speech" and "string = Go do it". If you want to, erase "You forgot to set a speech" and "Go do it", and replace them with whatever desired. Optional: under "string", it should say "wait = #". Change each one to "wait = 2". After finishing, start the game normally and spawn Maxwell's Head into the game. It should say whatever was typed in the file.
