Beefalo Wool is an item obtained by either killing a Beefalo or a Baby Beefalo, by shaving a Beefalo with a Razor while it sleeps, or within certain Set Pieces. It is used to make a Beefalo Hat, Puffy Vest, Winter Hat, and Carpeted Flooring.

Beefalo Wool also makes an excellent fuel for Campfires and Fire Pits, as it's very easy to get, and it provides 90 seconds of light for each piece of wool. A small herd of Beefalo can easily provide enough fuel for days of constantly burning Fire Pits.

Reign of Giants

In the Reign of Giants DLC there is an alternative method of getting Beefalo Wool, by picking Tumbleweeds.
