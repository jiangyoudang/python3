Ice is an item found in the Reign of Giants DLC by mining Mini Glaciers. It can be used to craft various temperature reducing items, added to an Ice Cube to repair it, eaten to reduce the player's temperature over 7.5 seconds, or used to extinguish fire and smoldering objects. It should also be taken in to account that Ice will never melt when put in an Ice Box, but will melt much faster when near a fire. When melting completely it will raise the player's Wetness

Ice is also used in a few Crock Pot recipes.

Despite the fact that Ice is not exactly food, eating it when it is nearly melted will still result in sanity loss.
