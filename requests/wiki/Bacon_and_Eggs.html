Bacon and Eggs are a Meat Food item cooked in the Crock Pot. They are made by adding a Tallbird Egg or 2 regular Eggs plus an amount of meat with a total value of 1.5 or more, and no Vegetables. It takes 40 seconds in the Crock Pot to cook this item.

It should be noted that putting more than 1 Monster Meat has a 50% chance of resulting in Monster Lasagna, unless twigs are added.

A fast way to obtain Bacon and Eggs is to find a Tallbird Nest, take the Tallbird Egg, and kill the Tallbird. That provides two Meat and the Tallbird Egg, and twigs can be added as a filler. The way to avoid Monster Lasagna is to use a Tallbird Egg with 2 Monster Meat and 1 Twigs, since Monster Lasagna cannot be made with Twigs.
