Grass Turf

Fuel Value 90/45 sec
Dropped by Grasslands tiles (Pitchfork)
Stacks up to 10
DebugSpawn "turf_grass"
“ I think I might be allergic to this. ”

–Woodie

Grass Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging Grassland tiles with a Pitchfork. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as a great fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit.
