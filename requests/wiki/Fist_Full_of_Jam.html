Fist Full of Jam is a Fruit food item cooked in a Crock Pot by combining any Fruits with a total value of 2.5 or less, except Dragon Fruit which will lead to Dragonpie. Having a total Fruits value of 3 or greater will create Fruit Medley instead. It takes 10 seconds to cook.

No Meats or Vegetables or Twigs should be added; otherwise it will result in Meatballs, Ratatouille, or Wet Goop respectively.

Usage

Trivia

  • Eating the individual ingredients cooked is almost always better than using them to make Fist Full of Jam, unless the player needs that small sanity boost (or want to make use of a Durian without losing any sanity) or needs the food to keep longer. Jam keeps for 15 days, compared to Berries' six and Roasted Berries' meager three.
    • Another possible reason to do this is to freshen a pile of Berries to be effective whilst using Wickerbottom. This works with spoiling Berries, meaning you can gain sanity and the full amount of intended hunger.
