A Batilisk Wing is a meat food item dropped by Batilisks when killed. It can be cooked to make a Cooked Batilisk Wing or dried on a Drying Rack for 2 days to give Small Jerky. It can be traded to the Pig King for a Gold Nugget. However, unlike most meats, neither raw nor cooked wings may be used in the Crock Pot. It is also an essential item in crafting the Bat Bat.

Cooked Batilisk Wing

Cooked Batilisk Wing is made by cooking a Batilisk Wing over a Campfire, Fire Pit or Star. It cannot be used in the Crock Pot. However, it can be given to Pig King for a Gold Nugget. When cooked, the amount of health and hunger that it restores increases slightly, sanity penalty is eliminated, and spoils slower.
