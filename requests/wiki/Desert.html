The Desert biome is found in the world of the Reign of Giants DLC. Commonly found in this biome are Hound Mounds, Tumbleweeds, Cacti, Buzzards, and Boulders. Occasional are the Spiky Trees, Spiky Bushes, and Grass Tufts. Desert is mostly covered with Sandy Turf.

Deserts are commonly found as a standalone biome at the edge of the world, rarely manifesting itself in the mainland between other biomes. This is the only other location where you can find Hound Mounds and Tumbleweeds.
