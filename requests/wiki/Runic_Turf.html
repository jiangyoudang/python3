Runic Turfs are turfs that can only be found in a Ruins. Unlike any other turf, these turfs cannot be acquired nor can be put in the inventory because their item counterpart does not exist. Although it can be remove from the ground using a Pitchfork. These turfs have three different textures and colors according to their appearance on the map, namely: White, Purple, and Dark.

These Turfs are one of the best indicator to identify the Nightmare State in a Ruins because the cracks on these turfs glow pink.
