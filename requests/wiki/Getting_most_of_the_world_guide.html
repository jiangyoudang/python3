    Base camp

    This is about to become the main part of the world. Make a base camp where you would like it to be. You can read the base camp guide for more help.

    Travelling

    As written in the base camp guide, set up your base camp near the road. It's good to be cobblestone, since this will lead you to pig villages, one of which can be with the king pig, which makes the gold infinite source. Other way is to settle near wormhole, which is good way to explore to most parts of the world. If you have the summons mod by heavenfall, you can craft chaotic wormholes to explore. Also trails are not bad.

    Farmings

    Each part of the world can be used to do different farms and things. For example, if you find some tallbird nests, you can use this to farm tallbird eggs. Make sure to make ways to these places.

    You can also farm rabbits, by placing a lot of traps near rabbit holes.

    This is very useful for the first couple winters, as live rabbits don't spoil (unless Reign of Giants is enabled) and can be used for meatballs and other Crock Pot foods.

    Using natural ways for your own

    You can use trails as natural way, and make cobblestone patches if you can find good things there. 

    Manures, silk, grass, MATERIALS!

    Use good places to make spider farms, beefalo pens, panic rooms, vegetable farms, grass, sapling and berry bushes gardens, smalll forests and so on. Make small camps (not big) on each place to have place to sleep if night is coming and you don't have enough time to return to your base.

    Spider farms

    Destroy spider dens and if you find spider queens. Gather spider eggs. Find a nice place to plant 4-5 nests. Build pig houses if you can(playing as Wendy is good since you can order Abigail to deal with the spiders). Then the pigs will attack the spiders so every time you come, you can find spider loot or pig loot if the spiders kill the pigs.     Beefalo pens

    Kill beefalos when you find until you get beefalo horn(this can take some time). You should build a pen in a nice place where to lead the found beefalos when you play the horn. Then leave the beefalos. Each time you come here there will be manure for you. Also having miner hat during  the night when beefalos sleep will be good for gathering beefalo wool. Craft yourself beefalo hats or avoid coming here during the beefalo mating season.

    Gardens

    Find three good places. Collect some saplings, berry bushes and grass tufts. You should collect some manure from your beefalo pens before proceeding. Then plant the grass tufts on the place you selected for them, same for berry bushes and saplings. Bring with you some tooth traps to set around the berry bushes to kill gobblers easy. Now you can collect grass, saplings and berries within couple of days.
