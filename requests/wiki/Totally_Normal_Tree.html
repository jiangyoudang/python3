The Totally Normal Tree is a tree found in the Forest Biome. It can be chopped down, to give the player 2 Living Logs. The player can then use a Shovel or Regal Shovel to dig up the stump to gain an additional Living Log. If burned, it will drop Charcoal when chopped just like other burned trees. These trees will have a randomized number of Evil Flowers growing near them. When cutting it down, it will make a groaning/moaning noise with every chop.

Bugs

  • Burning a Totally Normal Tree Stump will turn into a Burnt Totally Normal Tree instead of dropping an Ash.
