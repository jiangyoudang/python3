Light Flowers are plants that serves as a source of light that can normally be found in Caves and Ruins. They are generated all over caves but mostly in Cave Swamp and Wilds in the Ruins. The Light Bulb(s) can be picked from Light Flowers, and they can be used in crafting and refueling of a Lantern and Miner Hat. There are three types of light flowers, each yielding a different amount of Light Bulbs.

Behaviour

The Light Flower is a small light source, capable of protecting the player from Charlie's attacks. It ceases to act as a light source once picked. The double- and triple-bulb plants yield 2 and 3 bulbs respectively when picked. It takes 3 days for a flower with one bulb to regrow, 4-5 days for a two-bulbed flower, and 6 days to spawn a three-bulbed flower.

Light Flowers are unable to be relocated elsewhere with a shovel, and are thus limited to the Caves and Ruins.

Trivia

  • Light Flowers were added in the Underground update.
