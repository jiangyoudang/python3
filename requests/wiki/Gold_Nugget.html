A Gold Nugget is a mineral item which can be found by mining Boulders with gold veins on their side, by mining Stalagmites in Caves, or received from the Pig King when fed certain foods or given Gravedigger Items. Gold Nuggets can also be found lying on the ground in Graveyards or in Rockyland biomes, and they can also fall down from the ceiling in Caves during randomly occurring Earthquakes.

Gold Nuggets are used to create many items such as a Luxury Axe and Science Machine. Like all mineral items, they are food sources for Slurtles and Snurtles. Slurtles will attack any player holding Gold Nuggets if there aren't any other minerals in the vicinity.

Trivia

  • Gold nuggets take on a silvery luster when the player is insane.
