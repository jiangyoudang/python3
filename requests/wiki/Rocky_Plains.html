Rocky Plains

Abundant
Rare


The Rocky Plains is an environment that can be found inside a cave. These areas are a place where Rock Lobsters can be found and serve as their habitat. Just like the Stalagmite Biome, the entire area is covered by two types of turfs, namely Cave Rock Turf and Guano Turf . There are no known plants that can be found in these biomes.
