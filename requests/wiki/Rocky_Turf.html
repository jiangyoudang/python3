Rocky Turf

Fuel Value 90/45 sec
Dropped by Rockyland tiles (Pitchfork)
Stacks up to 10
DebugSpawn "turf_rocky"
“ SOME ROCKS ARE BETTER THAN OTHERS ”

–WX-78

Rocky Turf is one of the Turf items acquired by digging Rockyland tiles with a Pitchfork. Like other Turfs, it can be placed on any bare ground tile, or it can be used as great fuel in a Campfire or Fire Pit. It can also be used to craft Cobblestones, which are placeable Roads.

Berry Bushes, Saplings, Grass Tufts, Spiky Bushes, and Evergreens cannot be planted on Rocky Turf. Eyeplants can't spawn on this turf, so using it is a great way to farm Lureplants safely while not spending any more resources than those used to craft the Pitchfork.
