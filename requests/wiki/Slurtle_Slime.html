Slurtle Slime is a Mob-dropped item. It is produced by Slurtles and Snurtles after eating Rocks, and also dropped when they are killed.  It is very explosive, and can trigger earthquakes when ignited. It deals 50 damage per charge (It can be exploded in stacks). It can be a very good alternative to Gunpowder and can be easily farmed by feeding Slurtles. Additionally, it can be used to refuel a Lantern or a Miner Hat by 9.5%.

Trivia

  • This item may be explosive because Slurtles and Snurtles consume Nitre, an ingredient for high explosives like Gunpowder.
  • When the player becomes insane, Slurtle Slime will appear to be grayish blue.
