Sticky Webbing

Fuel Value 45 sec (1.5 segments)
Dropped by Spider Den
Stacks up to 10

Sticky Webbing is a Turf item. It is a creep-like substance that "flowed" out of Spider Dens, and would slow all creatures that stepped on it, except spiders. Unlike other Turfs, Sticky Webbing cannot dug up with a Pitchfork, although was once possible.

Sticky Webbing can also be found in Sacred and Labyrinth biomes in Ruins. The only differences are it has no den and its being guarded by Dangling Depth Dwellers.

After the "Doorway to Adventure" update, stepping on the Sticky Webbing will cause some spiders to emerge from the nest to investigate. The spiders will only go a short distance from the nest before returning. If the spider den is destroyed, the webbing will disappear.
