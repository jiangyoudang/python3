Animals are Mobs found throughout the world and are typically not hostile unless provoked (with the exception of Beefalo in heat and hungry Smallish Tallbirds). Some Animals are passive and will never attack. They simply flee from danger.

Some of the creatures below will raise a player's naughtiness rating when directly killed.

The Pig King is the only passive mob that cannot be attacked or killed.
