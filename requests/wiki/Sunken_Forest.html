Sunken Forest

Occasional
Rare

Sunken Forest is a Biome that can only be found inside a Cave. A combination of Grass Turfs and Forest Turfs covers the ground, and there is fog in the area. There are Plants that can be found in this area such as Saplings, Grasses, Berry Bushes, and Evergreens. These Plants thrive through the shaft of light that comes from the Surface World. There are no known Mobs that can naturally be found in this Biome, though Gobblers can be spawned by picking the Berry Bushes there.
