You will need:

  • Any Weapon (Spear or Tentacle Spike is best)
  • Log Suit or Football Helmet (optional but recommended)
  • Bird trap unlocked
  • Spider Eggs
  • Twigs
  • Seeds (optional)
  • Honey or Bee Boxes (optional)

Steps

  1. Locate a Pig Village with a Pig King.
  2. Plant Spider Eggs near Pig Village.
  3. Startle Spiders, and let the Pigs kill them. Loot dead spiders and pigs as quickly as possible.
  4. Give the collected Meat to the Pig King to receive gold , or eat it.
  5. Give the collected Monster Meat to 1-2 Pigs. They will turn into Werepigs and fight normal pigs . Collect their loot and give it to the Pig King .
  6. Use the collected Silk to craft Bird Traps and hunt Birds. Using seeds will enhance the process.
  7. Give Morsel to the pig king , and collect Gold Nuggets.

OR

  1. Dig lots of Gravedigger Items from Graves with Chester.
  2. Kill Blue Hounds for Blue Gems at Walrus Camp in Winter.
  3. Go to the Pig Village, and give all the collected items to the Pig King.

Notes

  • Players may preserve some Honey and Monster Meat; they will be cooked in Crock Pot and Honey Nuggets is made, which is good for healing .
  • Silk is required for many useful Survival Items; the player will be able to get crafting materials from spiders killed.

Alternative method

Build a birdcage , feed the bird cooked Monster Meat. This will yield one Egg. The Egg can be given the Pig King in exchange for one Gold Nugget. If the Monster Meat from Hounds isn't enough, additional Monster Meat can be obtained in various ways. One of the easiest ways is roaming the Marshes and cleaning up after battles between Tentacles, Merms and Spiders (this can easily be combined with collecting Reeds and Tentacle Spikes).
